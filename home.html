NEW SERVER CONTENT!

Following the late release of AO2 comes the beloved feature of making custom themes for the client. As a result of the freedom of custom content creation, fan-made client skins and assets are abound. One such overwhelmingly popular theme is offered by the Danganronpa content servers.

As such, we would like to welcome and express our gratitude to the diehard Danganronpa community members who have shared their works to increase the overall content of Attorney Online and set a high standard for content projects to come.

We would also like to express our gratitude to Loki (Jerm70), whose status updates about the DR client have continued incessantly. (Check out his other work down by the COMMUNITY MODS page!)

NEW WEBSITE LAUNCHED!

After a few days of conceptualizing what AO2 is really about, we were able to build up a new website from scratch. We hope to make this website bigger and better so please do not hesitate to share your feedback, comments and suggestions for the site HERE!
